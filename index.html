Welcome to the fast-paced and exciting game of "Catch the rooster"! In this game, your goal is to click on the roosters that appear on the screen as quickly as possible. But beware, these roosters are quick and will constantly be moving around, so you'll need to keep your wits about you if you want to win big. Each time you successfully hit a rooster, you'll earn points that will add up over time. But be careful - if you're too slow or miss the rooster entirely, the computer will swoop in and steal your potential points. So get ready to ruffle some feathers and show off your lightning-fast reflexes. Are you up for the challenge of "Catch the rooster"? Let's find out!
